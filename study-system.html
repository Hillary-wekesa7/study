The System Rules
01Never start a session without knowing exactly what you're studying. Read the task before you open the book.
02Active recall only — no passive re-reading. Close the notes and write what you remember. Then check.
03Past questions are the exam. Do them first to know what matters, then study backward from the gaps.
04Teach it out loud. If you can't explain it without notes, you don't know it well enough yet.
05Anatomy and physiology get double time. Biochemistry gets consistent daily exposure — not cramming.
06Critical thinking and faith & healing are high ROI — less content, easier marks. Never skip them.
07Sleep is studying. The consolidation that happens at night is non-negotiable. 10 PM is not optional.
08A bad session still counts. Show up even on low-energy days. Consistency beats intensity.
